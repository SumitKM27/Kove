Frequently Asked

Why does a custom mechanical keyboard matter?

A standard office keyboard is made of thin hollow plastic shells and cheap rubber dome sheets. A custom mechanical keyboard is built using heavy aluminum or hardwood cases, dense noise-dampening foams, lubed stabilisers, and premium mechanical switches. This results in a smooth typing feel, zero finger fatigue, and a satisfying, quiet thock sound profile.
